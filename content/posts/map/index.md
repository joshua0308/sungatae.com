I've been wanting to create a map that I can share with friends when they ask for recommendations. As I was curating this list, I realized that the places I like aren't necessarily the ones with the best food or drinks. I like small businesses, spots that have local regulars and where I had good memories with people. These places are personal to me and you can read more about each in the notes I added on the map https://sungatae.com/map.
